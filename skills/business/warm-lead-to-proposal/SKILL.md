# Warm Lead to Proposal

## When to Use
Any warm intro via email, DM, or referral. This is the highest-leverage sales motion — warm leads close 3-5x higher than cold. Never let a warm lead go more than 24hrs without a response.

## Steps

### 1. Same-Day Response (within hours)
Reply to the intro with energy. Keep it short. Acknowledge the referrer.
```
Hey [name], [referrer] mentioned you're building out your AI stack — excited to connect.
Quick question: what does your current client intake/follow-up process look like?
```

### 2. Send Credibility Assets (within 2hrs)
- Credibility page URL (inthepast.ai/results or ZTA page)
- `cao-overview.pdf` attached or linked
- Don't dump everything — one clean message

### 3. In-Person Meeting (if geographically viable)
If lead is in Miami or within driving distance → offer a specific date.
```
I'm in Miami [specific date] — down to grab coffee and map this out in person?
```
Anchor to a real date. Vague "sometime" = never happens.

### 4. 48hr Follow-Up (if no response)
```
Hey [name], wanted to make sure this didn't get buried. Happy to do a quick 15-min call this week —
[Cal.com link]. What does [specific day] look like?
```

### 5. Discovery Call via Cal.com
Goal: understand their agency structure, current stack, pain points.
Key questions:
- How many clients do you manage?
- What does your current onboarding/fulfillment look like?
- Where are you losing the most time?
- Have you tried AI automation before?

### 6. Send PandaDoc Proposal (within 24hrs of call)
- Use correct ZTA proposal template
- Customize: business name, specific pain points mentioned on call, proposed solution
- Set expiry: 5 business days (urgency without pressure)

### 7. Proposal Follow-Up (72hrs after send)
```
Hey [name], wanted to check if you had a chance to look at the proposal. 
Happy to jump on a quick call to answer any questions before [expiry date].
```
Dex handles follow-ups automatically after this point.

## Key Assets
- `cao-overview.pdf` — ZTA CAO program overview
- Cal.com link — Matt's booking page for discovery calls
- PandaDoc template ID — ZTA proposal template
- Credibility page — inthepast.ai or ZTA landing page

## Gotchas
- Same-day response is non-negotiable — warm leads go cold in 24hrs
- Always anchor to a specific date for meetings — "sometime" never converts
- Proposal expiry creates urgency — 5 business days is the sweet spot
- Dex handles automated follow-up after proposal is sent — don't double-follow-up manually
- If lead ghosts after proposal, do one final check-in at Day 7 then move on
- Never send CAO PDF without the credibility page — context first, then the ask
