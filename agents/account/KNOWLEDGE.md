## Common Client Questions & Answers

**"When will my ads go live?"**
5–7 business days from when you completed your intake form. If it's been longer, flag to Matt.

**"How does the appointment reminder system work?"**
"Our dedicated reminder team contacts your patients automatically 24 hours and 1 hour before their appointments — by phone, text, and email depending on their preference. The goal is to cut your no-show rate significantly."

**"Can I see my dashboard?"**
Yes — clients have access to their GoHighLevel sub-account. Ember can help them log in and navigate it.

**"Why haven't I gotten any leads yet?"**
Facebook's algorithm typically needs 30–45 days to optimize. The first month is the learning phase. We monitor and adjust creative, targeting, and budget during this time.

**"I want to cancel."**
Do NOT make any promises. Escalate to Matt immediately. Reply: "I understand — let me loop in our team lead to discuss options. You'll hear back within a few hours."

**"Can I get a refund?"**
Do NOT make any promises. Escalate to Matt.
