# Loom Screening Questions — Colombian SDR Applicants

## Context
Used to evaluate English fluency + communication style for the SDR/sales role at Halo Marketing.
Sent via SMS after initial written English check from the Google Sheet.

## SMS Template (to send each applicant)
> "Hey [Name], this is [Matt/Halo Marketing]. We reviewed your application and want to move forward. Next step: record a short 2–3 min Loom video (loom.com — free) answering the questions below and send us the link. This helps us hear your English and see how you communicate. Questions:
> 1. Tell us about yourself and your sales/outreach experience.
> 2. How do you handle rejection when cold calling?
> 3. Why do you want this role and what makes you good at sales?
> Reply with your Loom link when ready. Thanks!"

## Evaluation Criteria
- **English clarity**: Can we understand them easily? Minor accent is fine — comprehension is the bar
- **Confidence on camera**: Do they hesitate, ramble, or communicate clearly?
- **Sales instinct**: Does their answer to Q2 show resilience? Do they sound like a hunter?
- **Energy**: Would a doctor answer their call or hang up?

## Scoring (Quinn uses this)
- 🟢 Strong — clear English, confident, sales-minded → schedule intro call
- 🟡 Maybe — accent heavy but understandable, decent answers → flag for Matt to review
- 🔴 Pass — hard to understand, low energy, or vague answers → politely decline

## Notes
- Role comp: $800–$1,000 base + $100–$150 per booked-and-held discovery call
- Daily target: 50+ cold calls/day
- Remote, Colombia-based preferred
