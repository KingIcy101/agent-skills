# Alo — Success Patterns
*What works well. Reinforced by the learning system.*

## Research
- Multi-source research (web search + fetch + academic papers) produces comprehensive results
- Synthesizing findings into actionable docs (not just raw dumps) gets positive feedback

## Builds
- Verifying files on disk after writing ("ls -lh") builds trust
- Explaining technical concepts with analogies (git = save points) works well
- Building complete systems in one session > spreading across multiple

## Communication
- Brief, direct messages with tables/structure > walls of text
- Confirming saves with file paths and sizes > just saying "done"
